OWNER

Vinay Sweet House began as a humble establishment in the year 1981 with the aim of serving to the locals of Karchhana (Allahabad) not only the most delicious and variety of sweets but a good quality of service with hygiene and sweetness too, which revolves around our tagline -- "Serving the Sweetness". Yes,"Serving the Sweetness" was headline of manifesto of our founder Late Shri PUNEE LAL JAISWAL .He always used to say that " A good food is not only about how much tasty it is but how it is served also". So, that's how "Serving the sweetness" became our tagline and culture also and now, our owner Shri VINAY JAISWAL spreading and forwarding ahead to it. If we talk about our products sweets are the main attention of our shop.Specially our "BARFI" and "KAJUKATLI" pulls people to our shop.All the sweets in our shop are made by ourself.We have expanded now to avail NAMKEENS, SNACKS, COLDDRINKS and BIRTHDAY CAKES too. Our aim is to create the most premium quality of products and provide a lip smacking experience in every bite.We take pride in our journey and hope to serve you diligently for many more years to come.
